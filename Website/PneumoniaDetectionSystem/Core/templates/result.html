Interesting Facts About Lungs

  • The lungs are the only organs in the human body that can float on water.
  • An average person breathes in about 11,000 liters of air every day.
  • The surface area of the lungs is roughly the same size as a tennis court.
  • The left lung is smaller than the right lung to accommodate the heart.

Tips for Healthy Lungs

  1. Avoid smoking and exposure to secondhand smoke.
  2. Exercise regularly to improve lung function.
  3. Maintain a healthy diet rich in fruits and vegetables.
  4. Avoid exposure to air pollutants and toxins.
  5. Practice deep breathing exercises to strengthen the lungs.
